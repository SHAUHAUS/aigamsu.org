Welcome to the 2024 GLITCH National Student Design Competition.

Born through the power of a mishap, an unavoidable accident, a glitch will somehow almost always produce our best work. When you are forced to take a step back and reevaluate, you gain a new perspective. GLITCH celebrates that.


Awards will be announced at the awards reception on April 04, from 5-6pm. Awards will also be announced on social media following the reception and the following day.
